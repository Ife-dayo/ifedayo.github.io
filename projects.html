Bellabeat Marketing Analysis

Bellabeat is a high-tech manufacturer of health-focused products for women. They are a successful small company but they, but they have the potential to become a larger player in the global smart device market. In a bid to increase their marketing startegies, and recognize important trends in consumers' usage, data was gathered from 30 customers. The dataset was gotten from the marketing director after tracking the device usage of 30 users. During the exploratory analysis, I was able to gather findings about our users and how they use the device. These insights will be used to improve Bellabeat's marketing strategies.

WeRateDogs Twitter Analysis

WeRateDogs is a Twitter account that posts funny comments and rates of dogs from photos submitted by their followers. For this data analysis project, data was gathered from a number of sources, including WeRateDogs twitter account using Twitter API, and requests library. The aim was to have consolidated data about the tweets, the likes and retweets, and image predictions of the dogs posted.

Brazilian Hospital Patients

This dataset collects information from 100k medical appointments in Brazil and is focused on the question of whether or not patients show up for their appointment. We perform EDA to answer questions like:

  1. are reminders helpful?
  2. do scholarships encourage people more?
  3. what other factors are important for us to know in order to predict if a patient will show up for their scheduled appointment?
